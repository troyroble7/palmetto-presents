Partner Excellence Framework

LightReach Channel Partner Program

Tiers are earned, not given. Performance across 6 KPIs determines your standing. Only the best earn Platinum.

Program Philosophy

The LightReach Channel Partner Program is designed to reward partners for how they perform and what they earn. Unlike competitors who tier based on volume, LightReach tiers based on operational excellence. A smaller EPC with excellent first-time pass rates, collection rates, and NPS can earn Platinum over a high-volume EPC with sloppy operations.

"Status and tier are not given — they're earned through consistent execution and operational excellence."

— Troy Roble, SVP Business Development, Palmetto LightReach

The Four Tiers

Every LightReach partner starts as Certified and earns their way up through demonstrated performance. Tiers are reviewed twice annually (mid-year and year-end).

Certified
Entry Tier

Welcome to LightReach. Every new partner starts here. Access to core platform, training, and support.

Pro
Growth Tier

Demonstrated operational competence. Consistent quality metrics and growing volume. Dedicated RM assigned.

Elite
Performance Tier

Strong across all 6 KPIs. Access to premium products, business reviews, and market expansion support.

Platinum
Highest Standard in Solar

Only 13 EPCs in 2026. A rarified designation earned through exceptional operational excellence and customer experience.

6 Performance KPIs

Every partner is measured across six key performance indicators. These metrics determine tier standing, pricing access, and product eligibility.

# KPI What It Measures
1 Installation Quality First-time pass rate on milestone submissions. Clean job packets = fewer rejections = faster funding.
2 Aging Backlog How quickly projects move through the pipeline. Stuck projects tie up capital and hurt customer experience.
3 Customer NPS Net Promoter Score from post-install surveys. Happy customers pay on time and refer friends.
4 Collection Rate Percentage of customers making on-time payments. Directly impacts ABS portfolio health and cost of capital.
5 Tranche Execution How efficiently partners hit funding milestones. Predictable execution enables better financial planning.
6 Monthly Volume NTP volume as a measure of growth and commitment. Volume matters, but only alongside quality.

What Platinum Means

Only 13 LightReach Platinum EPCs exist in 2026. This is intentionally exclusive. Platinum represents the EPCs that LightReach trusts most with pipeline — the highest standard in the solar industry.

Platinum is earned through exceptional performance across all 6 KPIs: strong first-time pass rates, excellent customer collection rates, disciplined tranche execution, high NPS, clean backlog management, and sustained volume. It is the aspiration tier that all EPCs should work toward.

Why it matters for ABS: Platinum EPCs produce the cleanest assets. Their customers pay on time, their installations pass inspection the first time, and their NPS scores reflect genuine customer satisfaction. This directly supports LightReach's position as the tightest-priced solar ABS issuer in the market (225 bps — tighter than Sunrun, SunStrong, and GoodLeap).
