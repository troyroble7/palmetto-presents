# Accessing Your Palmetto Presents Partner Portal

## What is Palmetto Presents?

Palmetto Presents is your dedicated intelligence hub from the LightReach team. It contains interactive report decks tailored specifically to your organization — including operations performance, customer collections, NTP pipeline, post-install status, and NPS analysis.

## How to Access Your Portal

1. **Go to:** [palmetto-presents.vercel.app](https://palmetto-presents.vercel.app)
2. You'll see the Palmetto Presents homepage. Click on your EPC tile, or navigate directly to your portal URL (provided by your Relationship Manager).
3. You'll be prompted to **enter your passphrase** on the sign-in page.
4. Enter the passphrase provided to you by your LightReach Relationship Manager and click **Sign In**.
5. You'll land directly on your partner portal with all your reports accessible.

## Your Passphrase

- Your passphrase was provided to you by your LightReach Relationship Manager.
- It is unique to your organization — do not share it with other EPC partners.
- If you've lost your passphrase or need it reset, contact your Relationship Manager.

## What You Can Access

- **Your Partner Portal** — Your dedicated page with all reports specific to your organization.
- **Your Reports** — All interactive report decks (Operations, Collections, NTP, Post-Install, NPS) for your company.
- **Public Pages** — The Solar 3.0 explainer and Channel Program overview are available to everyone.

## What You Cannot Access

- Other EPC partner portals or reports
- Competitive intelligence reports (admin-only)

If you attempt to visit another partner's portal or the competitive intelligence section, you'll see an "Access Denied" page.

## Session Duration

- Your session lasts **7 days** from the time you sign in.
- After 7 days, you'll be asked to enter your passphrase again.
- You can sign out anytime by clicking the **Sign Out** link in the navigation bar.

## Troubleshooting

| Issue | Solution |
|-------|----------|
| "Invalid passphrase" error | Double-check for typos. Passphrases are case-sensitive. |
| Can't see the login page | Make sure you're going to the correct URL. |
| Reports not loading | Try refreshing the page. If the issue persists, contact your RM. |
| Need a new passphrase | Contact your LightReach Relationship Manager. |

## Questions?

Contact your LightReach Relationship Manager for any access issues or questions about your reports.
