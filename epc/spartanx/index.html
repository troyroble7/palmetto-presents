Competitive Landscape

Why LightReach Over the Alternatives

vs. Sunrun

Stock crashed 28-37%. NSV collapsed 30%. Cutting 40%+ affiliate volume. $14.7B debt, ~$1B/yr interest. Altman Z-Score -0.47. Their affiliate cuts create recruiting opportunity for LightReach EPCs like SpartanX.

vs. GoodLeap

Loan company trying to become TPO. All 3 rating agencies downgrading ABS. Retreating from FL & TX. FBI fraud investigation. Only 15 states, ~250 EPCs. LightReach: 31+PR, 700+ EPCs, tighter spreads.

vs. Participate Energy

Emerging TPO entrant without the ABS track record or capital markets depth of LightReach. No serial issuer status. No TCD media platform for lead generation. Limited product lease lines.

vs. Propel / Enphase

Equipment manufacturer entering finance — hardware-first, not finance-first. Tied to Enphase ecosystem. LightReach is equipment-agnostic, finance-native, with $1.136B ABS issuance and Wall Street's tightest spreads.
