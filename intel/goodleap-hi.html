GoodLeap Home Improvement Loan Business — Competitive Intelligence

Critical Distinction

  • GoodLeap's solar TPO business is retreating from FL/TX (Feb 2026 retreat announcement)
  • GoodLeap's home improvement loan business is ACCELERATING — this is their core business
  • Home improvement loans are the majority revenue driver, NOT solar

Business Scale

  • Revenue: ~$500M (Dec 2024 estimate, Latka)
  • Total financed: $32B+ since 2018 to 1M+ homeowners
  • Employees: ~1,300
  • Valuation: $12B (Oct 2021, last known)
  • Total ABS deals: 24 (as of Dec 2025)

Key Competitive Moats

  • 4,000+ active HI installers (grew from 1,000 in late 2024)
  • 40,000+ sales professionals on platform total
  • 50-state coverage (vs 32 markets)
  • 24-hour contractor payment after project completion
  • GoodLeap Payments — new payment processing product (Jun 2025) for roofing/HI

Product Categories (16+)

  • Dealer fees (20-35%) — inflate loan, create consumer anger, drive BBB complaints (1,076 in 3 years)
  • No maintenance/warranty — homeowner bears all repair risk
  • Debt on credit report — impacts DTI and credit profile
  • MN AG lawsuit ($35M alleged overcharges) + CFPB/FBI scrutiny on solar side
  • Solar ABS downgrades (KBRA, S&P, Fitch) create brand risk spillover
  • No long-term customer relationship — once loan is paid, GoodLeap is done
  • No cross-sell ecosystem — single-product financing, no media platform
