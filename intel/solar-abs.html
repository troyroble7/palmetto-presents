Loan ABS Distress Signals

  • Nov 2025 = worst month for solar loan ABS credit performance
  • Delinquencies at/near record highs since KBRA index inception
  • Unsecured solar loans: 4.8% delinquency vs 2.6% general personal
  • 53 rating downgrades on US solar ABS since mid-2024
  • Downgrades concentrated in non-prime auto and solar
  • 2023-24 vintages at peak loss levels
  • Sunnova re-aging inflated Dec 2025 numbers

Spreads & Pricing (Ohm Q3-25)

  • Sunrun 2025-3: 240 bps
  • SunStrong 2025-1: 235 bps (Class A-1: 5.95% coupon)
  • GoodLeap TIP 2025-1: 260 bps (widest)
  • GoodLeap loan (GDLP): 265 bps
  • Sunrun Mar 2025: 6.36% yield
  • Spreads remain tight by historical standards
  • Strong institutional demand; top-tier banks competing for mandates
  • ESG/sustainability mandates driving additional demand

OBBB Impact on ABS

  • 25D expiration = positive for lease/PPA ABS (TPO = only ITC pathway)
  • TPO share surging: 42% Q3-25, forecast 64% by 2026 (Ohm), potentially 75%+
  • Negative for loan ABS (no tax credit for cash buyers)
  • Safe harboring creates multi-year securitization pipeline (2027-2029)
  • Begin construction by Jul 3, 2026 for 4-year continuity

Competitor ABS Intelligence

  • SunStrong: $900M single deal (STRONG 2025-1). 64K assets, 528MW, FICO 763. Led by ATLAS SP (Apollo), ING, BofA, Citi.
  • GoodLeap: 24 total securitizations. Pivoting to TPO via TIP. $4.6B loan volume in 2025. Pulling out of FL/TX.
  • Sunnova: $6B in securitized assets remained stable through Ch.11 — bankruptcy-remote structures proven.
  • GoodFinch: >$5B in renewables ABS. Acquired Sunnova assets.
