SolarEdge Technologies (SEDG) — Market Intelligence (Feb 2026)

Company Snapshot

  • HQ: Herzliya, Israel | NASDAQ: SEDG | Market Cap: ~$2.1B
  • CEO: Shuki Nir (Dec 2024, turnaround operator, ex-SanDisk)
  • CFO: Asaf Alperovitz (Mar 2025)
  • Employees: ~3,041 (down 44% from ~5,400 after 4 rounds of layoffs)
  • Stock: ~$35 (52-wk: $11–$49 | down 91% from 2021 ATH $389)

Financial Recovery

  • FY 2025: $1.18B rev (+31% YoY), 16.6% GAAP GM, ($405M) net loss, +$77M FCF
  • Q4 2025: $335M rev, 23.3% non-GAAP GM, near breakeven ($8.2M non-GAAP loss)
  • Q1 2026 guide: $290-320M rev, 20-24% GM
  • Target: EBIT profitability H2 2026
  • Debt: ~$672M (convertible notes, refinanced)
  • Cash: ~$581M
  • Analyst consensus: 1 Buy, 22 Hold, 5 Sell. Median PT $33.

NA Residential Business

  • Ohm Q3-25: 22% inverter share (#3 behind Enphase 37% and Tesla 29%). WoodMac had rated #1 in Q2 2025, but Ohm's broader Q3 data shows Enphase still leads
  • #2 US residential battery supplier Q3 2025 (~15% storage share per Ohm)
  • US = 59% of Q4 revenue ($198M); NA revenue ~$650-700M FY2025
  • Products: Energy Hub inverters (7.6-11.4kW), S-Series optimizers, Energy Bank battery (9.7kWh, 94.5% RTE)
  • Nexis platform coming H2 2026 (modular click-stack LFP, 4.9kWh blocks, <15 min install)

US Manufacturing

  • Austin TX: Inverters (50K/quarter) | Seminole FL: Optimizers (ramping to 2M/quarter) | Utah: Batteries
  • Non-FEOC (Israeli company) — strongest domestic content path with MLPE
  • Safe harbor agreements with Sunrun + unnamed financier (Jan 2025)
  • Monetizing 45X tax credits ($40M+)

Key Risks

  • Reliability: 60%+ inverter replacement rates reported; poor Trustpilot reviews
  • Europe: 30% of revenue from declining market (contraction through 2027)
  • Chinese competition: 9 of top 10 global inverter suppliers are Chinese; SEDG is #7
  • Financial fragility: Altman Z-Score -1.28; 58% bankruptcy probability (Macroaxis)
  • Nexis execution risk (H2 2026 launch)

Competitive Position

  • $1,500-3,000 cheaper per system vs Enphase
  • DC-coupled = higher efficiency for solar+storage
  • 60% installer penetration vs 74% Enphase — trust gap
  • Tesla PW3 integrated inverter is a threat to both SEDG and Enphase
  • Ohm Q3-25 microinverter pricing: $0.435/Wac (-5% QoQ) — PW3 competition driving ALL inverter prices down
  • Enphase ADLP price increase (Feb 9, 2026): 10-20% on microinverters — may benefit SEDG's competitive position on price
